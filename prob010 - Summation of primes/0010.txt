Summation of primes
-------------------

The sum of the primes below 10 is 2 + 3 + 5 + 7 = 17.

Find the sum of all the primes below two million.
----------

NOTE : While in theory this can be done using brute force, even fast machines are too slow for this problem (they are good to 100K, but not 1M).

[2000000, 5.14] sec
